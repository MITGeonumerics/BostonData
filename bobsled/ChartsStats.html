Tally of Free Hours

Each parking meter had a particular number of free parking hours. Each of these hour ranges was added to a counter per day of the week. This was then done for all 6955 meters. The result is a count of the total hours free per day of the week. While the number is arbitary, the percentages reveal which day of the week has the most free parking. The chart expresses that Sunday is the best day for free parking, followed by Saturday, and then all the days of the week are tied.

Installation Dates

Each parking meter contained data on the date of installation. Of the meters examined, the majority were installed at the same time. A negligible amount contained no data on that property.

This analysis revealed that the meters are generally new. Both prominently featured installation dates are from 2017. However, it is probable that installation was gradual (the data likely refers to date of the project starting), as installing thousands of parking meters at the same time is hardly plausible.

Common Hour Ranges - How to use

The counts of common hours data reveals the hours mostly likely present on any given meter. Hence, if someone passing by a meter, they can know that there is a high likelihood of free parking if it's before 8am during the week!
